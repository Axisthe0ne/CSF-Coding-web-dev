Cheesy Garlic Bread

"The world's finest cheesy garlic bread recipe"

Axis' Recipes. The free recipe site!

Recipe List

  • Bread [preferably a firm & crusty loaf]
  • Any shredded cheese you prefer [preferably a cheese that melts]
  • Unsalted butter
  • Fresh herbs [parsley, black pepper]
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps to making the cheesy garlic bread

  1. The first step is to prepare your garlic butter spread by combining a stick of softened butter with your fresh minced garlic, and parsley, salt and black pepper into a bowl. Make sure to mix it until evenly distributed.

  2. Next is to prepare the loaf of bread, cutting the loaf in half, lengthwise, with a bread knife [if you own one].

  3. Slather the garlic butter onto the bread. Don't be afraid to use a good amount of it on the slice for the next step which will be

  4. Take your cheese of choice and grater it onto your buttered bread or into a seperate container to then sprinkle onto the bread. Now, you have your cheese'd garlic bread!

  5. Now, it's time to bake, so preheat your toaster-oven to 200 ℃. Placing your cheesy garlic bread into the oven for 20 minutes until it is melted.

  6. Once you're finished you can do the finishing touches. Slicing your garlic bread into nice bites-sized pieces. We reccomend to cut it into 8 even slices as seen in the example or as many slices as you need.

Conclusion!

And there you have it, a nice delicious cheesy garlic bread made crispy and with a gooey melt in your mouth texture. We hope the recipe is to your liking and you enjoy more
